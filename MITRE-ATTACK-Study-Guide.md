

## What is MITRE ATT&CK?
MITRE ATT&CK is a knowledge base of adversary behaviors organized into tactics and techniques. It helps security teams understand, detect, and respond to attacker activity.


## Tactics vs Techniques

Tactic - The attacker's goal
Technique - How the attacker accomplishes that goal

Tactic Examples: Initial Access- The advesary is trying to get into your network
                 Execution - The adversary is trying to run malicious code
                 Persistence - The adversary is trying to maintain their foothold on systems.(replacing or hijacking legitimate code)
                 Privelege Escalation - The adversary is trying to gain higher level permissions

Technique Example: Attackers utilize defense evasion techniques to avoid detection, disable security tools, hdie malicious activity, or make actions seem normal. The goal is to remain undetected while maintaining firm access on a system. 

Defense Evasion examples:
Clearing Windows event logs
Disabling antivirus or endpoint protection
Obfuscating PowerShell commands
Renaming malicious files to appear legitimate
Hiding processes or files
Deleting forensic evidence
